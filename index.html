6:50 – 8:00 PM · Evening Experience

Niagara Parks Power Station at Night Confirmed slot

7005 Niagara Pkwy · Map ↗ · Included in Wonder Pass

1905 hydroelectric station. Underground tunnel 180 feet below ground, 2,200 feet long, excavated by hand. Evening includes Currents, a sound and light show, then illuminated views of Horseshoe Falls.

Walk back along the Parkway, ~10 minutes from Table Rock.

After 8:00 PM · Walk Back

Falls Illumination

Horseshoe Falls is illuminated through late evening in May. Walk the Parkway back to the hotel.

If the rain has come in or the wind picks up, the balcony view is the equivalent payoff without the walk. Either works.

Friday · Three Falls Attractions, then NOTL

May 15, 2026
8:00 – 8:30 AM · Breakfast

Fallsview Buffet

In the hotel. Convenient before the boat tour. Falls views from the dining room.

9:30 – 11:00 AM · On the Water

Niagara City Cruises Buy tickets

5920 Niagara Pkwy · Map ↗

20-minute ride to the base of Horseshoe and American Falls. You will get wet. Ponchos provided but a dry change is better.

~11:15 AM

Check out · Sheraton Fallsview

Pack the night before. Load the car straight from the cruise return.

11:30 AM – 12:30 PM · Falls Attraction

Journey Behind the Falls Wonder Pass

6650 Niagara Pkwy · Map ↗

Elevator down 125 feet through the rock, then tunnels behind Horseshoe Falls to two portal viewpoints. Lower observation deck looks up at the falls from the base.

Same building as Thursday's dinner. Allow about an hour with the queue.

12:45 – 1:45 PM · Lunch

Tide and Vine Oyster House

3491 Portage Road · Map ↗

Seafood-forward, local and independent. The strongest standalone restaurant in the Falls area. ~7 minutes from Table Rock.

2:00 – 2:45 PM · Falls Attraction

White Water Walk Wonder Pass

4330 Niagara Pkwy · Map ↗

Elevator down to a quarter-mile boardwalk at the edge of the Class VI rapids in the Niagara Gorge. North of the Falls, in the direction of NOTL.

Tide and Vine to White Water Walk is ~10 minutes.

3:00 – 4:00 PM · Slow Drive North

Niagara Parkway to NOTL

Take it slow. The Parkway runs along the gorge and through wine country. Pull off where you want. ~25 minutes of actual driving, stretched across an hour to land at NOTL right at check-in.

Stay on the Parkway. Do not take the highway.

10:30 AM – 12:30 PM

Lock 3 / Welland Canals Centre

1932 Welland Canals Pkwy · Map ↗ · Free (donation-based)

The Welland Canal connects Lake Ontario to Lake Erie and bypasses Niagara Falls. Ships up to 740 feet transit the locks.

Check the ship passage schedule the day before on the museum's Facebook page. Time arrival around it. Allow 1.5 – 2 hours.

Niagara Escarpment

Leave NOTL via Ridge Road north toward Queenston. Follow the Escarpment west through Grimsby and Beamsville. Pull off at viewpoints for lake views, no commitment. Then QEW west to Pearson.
